How to Use OSIPI CAPLEX

Step 1: Create an Account

Sign up by clicking on the "Sign Up" button. Fill in your details and verify your email to start using our platform.

Step 2: Upload Your Images

Go to the "Upload" section to add your images. You can upload multiple files at once for processing.

Step 3: Review and Analyze

Access the "Dashboard" to review processed results and use the analysis tools to generate insights and reports.
